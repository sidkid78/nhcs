Four Coupled Solvers — Digital Twin
twin.py orchestrates llg_solver · q_tensor · fluid · photonics · coil_field
▼
① Coil Field (EM Solver)
coil_field.py · Biot-Savart + FEM
12-channel dodecahedral array: nested tri-axial Helmholtz + Maxwell pairs + RF micro-coils. Computes spatial B-field as function of 12 channel currents. Hard limit: B < 45 mT (above → Rosensweig instability). Target: ~28 mT stable.
② LLG Solver (Magnetization)
llg_solver.py · MuMax3 wrapper
Landau–Lifshitz–Gilbert dynamics for Fe₃O₄ ferromagnetic nematic colloid. Couples magnetization M with coil B-field. Drives formation of Hopfion spin texture. JAX reduced-order (64³ grid) used before full MuMax3 run.
③ Q-Tensor Solver (Liquid Crystal)
q_tensor.py · FEniCSx
Nematic Landau–de Gennes Q-tensor PDE. Models liquid-crystal director field coupled with magnetization. Handles topology changes (sphere→torus→double-torus) via defect-mediated transitions. Trickiest solver — genus-shifting is an order-parameter topology change.
④ Fluid + Photonic Solvers
fluid.py · photonics.py
Navier–Stokes (incompressible + active director stress) verifies Hopfion stability within viscosity window. FDTD / transfer-matrix maps Valence → emission wavelength 420–720 nm. Ensures no turbulence during reconfigure (<2s budget).
Differentiable path: Replace solvers with JAX-MD / JAX-CFD / Warp for end-to-end adjoint gradients → free adjoint-based control synthesis (target Hopfion → optimal coil currents). Buys you controller inversion without a separate optimizer.

▼
Forward inversion: target Hopfion → 12 coil currents
Adjoint
Given a target Hopf map from TPMM, the controller solves the inverse problem: what combination of 12 coil currents produces the target B-field configuration? Solved via adjoint methods or differentiable physics. The 6-axis Helmholtz array supports 50 kHz frequency modulation, 150 A/ms slew rate, sub-50ns signal latency via FPGA + Direct Digital Synthesis (DDS).
Sapphire Chamber Boundary Conditions
chamber.py
100mm monocrystalline sapphire (Al₂O₃) sphere, 5mm wall. >98.5% transmission 420–720nm. Zero-velocity boundary condition provides inward radial pressure preventing vortex rings from expanding. Grade-5 Titanium cage withstands 250N oscillating magnetic load. Micro-channel water cooling prevents eddy-current thermal drift.
RetargetRequest handler (lateral from L3)
Lateral
Layer 3 PID may request frequency adjustments if CLI overshoots. The controller intercepts these RetargetRequest messages and adjusts coil currents accordingly — BUT only within 15–40 Hz (Pydantic validator hard-clamps at field level). L3 cannot trigger turbulent destruction or viscous diffusion regardless of how extreme a CLI spike is.

metrics.py — closes outer loop back to Layer 1 merit priors
▼
BHI — Biometric Homeostasis Index ∈ [0,1]
Stability
Measures how well the subject maintained physiological equilibrium during the concept delivery. High BHI = user stayed in flow state throughout. Low BHI = significant autonomic disruption (spikes in GSR, HRV crash). Feeds back to L1 as evidence that this topology class is biologically tolerable.
SDR — Semantic Distance Reduction ∈ [0,1]
Integration
Measures how much the conceptual distance between the delivered topology and the user's existing semantic model was reduced by the session. High SDR = successful integration (the "alien" became familiar). The key empirical unknown: does high SDR mean genuine conceptual uptake, or just well-tuned synesthetic VR? Cannot be resolved computationally — requires real participants.
